# Momentum AI — Hackathon Demo Script & Presentation Guide

This guide is designed for the Pitch Lead and Technical Lead of Momentum AI. It contains our complete 3-minute hackathon demo script, slide outlines, and critical presentation talking points to capture judge interest and secure a win.

---

## 🚀 The Slide Outline (Slide Deck Presentation)

### Slide 1: Title & Hook
- **Visual**: Glassmorphic, dark theme background with high-contrast text "Momentum AI".
- **Headline**: *The Autonomous Chief of Staff for High-Performance Founders.*
- **Talking Points**: "Frictionless task tracking is broken. Founders spend 40% of their day managing tasks, scheduling calendars, and fighting burnout instead of building products. Meet Momentum AI."

### Slide 2: The Problem (Burnout & Friction)
- **Visual**: Overlapping calendars, red warning icons representing "Cognitive Fatigue" and "Deadline Drift".
- **Headline**: *Calendar chaos leads to project failure.*
- **Talking Points**: "Current tools like Todoist are static logs. They don't adapt to scheduling conflicts, they don't predict when you are at risk of missing deadlines, and they don't help you transition into deep focus."

### Slide 3: The Solution (Momentum AI Architecture)
- **Visual**: Clean architecture diagram showcasing our 7-agent cognitive model powered by Gemini 3.5.
- **Headline**: *An AI co-pilot that coordinates your life.*
- **Talking Points**: "Momentum AI isn't just an organizer. It is a multi-agent system that digests raw notes, builds interactive dependency graphs, predicts delivery drift, and optimizes your calendar dynamically."

---

## 🎤 The Live Demo Script (3-Minute Timeline)

### Step 1: Beautiful Onboarding & Seamless Sign-In (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Action**: Load the clean, high-contrast dashboard. Showcase the glassmorphism aesthetic. Log in via Google Single Sign-On (Oauth).
- **Pitch**: "We start with a frictionless onboarding experience. We authenticate securely via Google, pulling in our core email and calendar profiles. No complex forms—just immediate alignment."

### Step 2: Voice Dictation & Natural Language Processing (0:45 - 1:30)
- **Action**: Click the "Voice Input" or text draft area. Type: `"Need to finalize the Series A presentation by Wednesday at 3pm, high priority, requires completing market research first."` Click parse.
- **Pitch**: "Instead of manual database inputs, I simply dictate a sentence. In real-time, our Gemini model parses this raw string into structured task data, recognizes dependencies (`market research`), calculates priority, and generates a detailed subtask checklist."

### Step 3: Interactive Dependency Graph & Risk Forecasting (1:30 - 2:15)
- **Action**: Hover over the visual "Task Dependency Graph". Show the predicted drift risk scores.
- **Pitch**: "Look at our visual workspace. Our system doesn't just list tasks—it builds a live interactive tree showing how items are related. It runs an autonomous risk model estimating how likely you are to drift past a deadline based on current meeting loads, cognitive blocks, and subtask density."

### Step 4: Autonomous Calendar Optimization & buffering (2:15 - 3:00)
- **Action**: Click "Optimize Calendar". Watch the schedule feed generate focus reservation blocks with 15-minute decompression gaps automatically.
- **Pitch**: "Now, watch this magic. I click Optimize. Our Scheduler Agent reads my active list, maps out blocking times, schedules deep-work focus reserves, and embeds 15-minute decompressing periods so I never transition straight from a heavy research block to a high-stakes presentation. This is the future of human-AI collaboration."
